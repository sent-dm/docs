## Features

Here are some of the features that Sent offers:

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Channel Identification" icon="message">
    Sent automatically identifies a contact's primary messaging channel with just a phone number. Sent also manages alternative / fallback communication channels to ensure deliverability.
  </Accordion>

<Accordion title="Easy Personalization" icon="pencil">
    Sent lets you create message templates that you can reuse. You can also personalize templates by adding dynamic fields and media components.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Content Transformation" icon="code">
    Sent automatically configures and formats your templates based on the schema requirements of each messaging channel, ensuring seamless cross-channel compatibility and recipient experiences.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Advanced Analytics" icon="chart-line">
    Sent provides powerful analytics for your contacts and messages, including opens, read receipts, media/link interactions, as well as on-site events.
</Accordion>


</AccordionGroup>
